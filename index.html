About The TechNora

TechNora is a national-level inter-college tech fest conducted by the Department of Computer Science and Applications (DCSA) at Safi Institute of Advanced Study in Vazhayur. The event was first launched on March 22, 2022, and featured a variety of tech events and expo stalls. This year, the DCSA of Safi is organizing TechNora 2.0 as a big, nationwide event to showcase technology all over the nation.
